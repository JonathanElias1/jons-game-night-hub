Lifelines: Peek (ask Cody), Copy (copy Cody), Hint, Save (auto on first wrong)
Keys: 1-4 = answer, H = hint, B = back to board, R = restart

Grade 1 (100 pts each)

SubjectQuestionAnswerHint
Language Arts What is the verb in the sentence "Jon saved my life"? C) saved Verbs are action words. Which word tells what Jon did?
Math The event starts at 12:27 PM. Kaz texts 'almost ready' at 12:18 PM. She takes 56 minutes to get ready and 13 minutes to drive. What time will Kaz arrive? A) 1:27 PM Ignore the start time (just like Kaz). Use addition.

Media Jon records video by attaching a 28-70mm lens to which item? A) Camera body It's the part the lens snaps onto, the one with the record button.

SubjectQuestionAnswerHint
